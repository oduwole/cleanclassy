Pest Control

Pest infestation is a very troublesome issue that can pose serious health risks to you and other people in pest infested areas.
Pest infestations also cause serious damage and deterioration to physical structures/properties

Our professional approach to handling all pest infestations involves maintaining the standard quality and service obtainable and required in the industry.
Our eco-friendly pest control solutions ensure that you and the environment are not exposed to harmful toxins and chemicals.

We have the knowledge, expertise and equipment to eradicate pests from different kinds of vicinities.

  • Integrated pest management
  • Fumigation
  • Deratisation
  • Roach control
  • Reptile control
  • Insect control (mosquito, ants, flies, termites, bed bugs)
