Laundry and Dry-cleaning

Many methods of cleaning have rendered garments useless or less desirable because of the inappropriateness of these methods,
While some clothing needs to be dry-cleaned, some others need to be laundered and this is where we pride ourselves on providing top-notch laundry and dry cleaning service.

We employ proper, thorough and standardized processes in getting your clothes clean and fit to adorn. Our professional laundry and dry-cleaning services are built on innovation, technology, and years of experience.
We ensure your clothing is taken care of at the highest level of care and our processes meet the highest verifiable standard in the industry.

All clothing in our care are dealt with by our well-trained and experienced dry cleaning experts.
You need not worry about misplaced, damaged or mishandled clothing as our standard processes guide against such issues.

Our unique approach starts with a detailed inspection of every clothing collected and proper sorting of each clothing before going through the dry-cleaning process. Each process is customized and well suited for each type of clothing.

There is no gainsaying that we handle every of your clothing in toxin-free solvents to prevent any possible damage to your clothing and ensure the highest quality of dry cleaning is always delivered.

Our overall laundry and dry cleaning services cover:

  1. Laundry
  2. Steam press ironing
  3. Garment repair
  4. Stain removal
  5. Special garments (wedding dresses, customized clothing etc.)
